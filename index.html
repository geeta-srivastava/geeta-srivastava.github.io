About

I'm a PhD researcher at NIT Sikkim, specialising in AI-driven control systems for autonomous vehicles — from underwater robots (AUVs) to drones (UAVs). My work bridges classical control theory with modern machine learning: building controllers that are not just optimised on paper, but validated on real hardware.

Before academia, I spent four years at Tata Consultancy Services across API engineering, EU medical device regulatory affairs, and 3D medical imaging — which gave me a strong sense of how rigorous technical work translates into real-world impact.

Currently deepening my expertise in PyTorch, computer vision (Self-Organizing Maps for robotic dynamics), and RAG-based search systems. I care about building things that are both mathematically sound and actually useful.
